Pragyan

I created a natural language based Question answering engine called "Pragyan", as my final year project as part of my under graduation course.

I used DBpedia as the data source, used SPARQL to retrieve the data and created a small NLP parser that converts natural language question to a SPARQL query and retrieve the data.
